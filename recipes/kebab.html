Kebab recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • beef
  • onion
  • garlic
  • loaf

Steps

  1. Meat: 1 to 2 lbs of ground beef (80/20) or ground lamb. (You can also use boneless, skinless chicken thighs marinated in yogurt and tomato paste).
  2. Binders: 1–2 tablespoons of Greek yogurt (for moisture) and 1 grated onion.
  3. Seasoning & Spices: Minced garlic, salt, black pepper, ground cumin, smoked paprika, dried oregano, and ground coriander.

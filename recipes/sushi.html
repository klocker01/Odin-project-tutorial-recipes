Sushi recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 cooked shrimp tempura pieces (frozen & baked works great for speed)
  • 1 cup cooked sushi rice (seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt)
  • 1 sheet Nori (seaweed), cut in half
  • 1/2 avocado or cucumber, sliced thin

Steps

  1. Prep the mat: Wrap a bamboo rolling mat in plastic wrap (stops the rice from sticking).
  2. Layer: Place half a sheet of nori on the mat. Press a thin, even layer of sushi rice across the entire sheet. Flip it over so the rice faces down.
  3. Fill: Line up 2 shrimp tempura pieces and your avocado/cucumber slices along the center of the nori.
  4. Roll: Roll tightly from the bottom up, using the mat to shape it into a snug cylinder.

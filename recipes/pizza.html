Pizza recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups (8 oz) low-moisture mozzarella, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat up: Preheat your oven to its maximum temperature—500°F (260°C) or higher.
  2. Shape: Stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle on a baking sheet dusted with cornmeal.
  3. Assemble: Spread sauce over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border. Top with Parmesan, then the mozzarella.
